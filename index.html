RAKHI ROY HALDER

Educationist,Author and Social Scientist

Rakhi Roy Halder, Associate Professor of Loreto College, Kolkata, is an author, life skill coach, and amateur visual artist. She has organized unique research programs sponsored by the Ministry of Culture (Government of India) and introduced the concept of 'Life Oriented Interdisciplinary Research' in the 35th Indian Social Science Congress.

She is the founder of Stress-Free Lifestyle Hub, which helps apply quantum science theories in life to gain a productive and stress-free lifestyle

She is the author of the books 'Myth breaker', 'Mystic Love' & 'Secrets of Creation,' which reveal the relation between the quantum world and human destiny. Her books are available worldwide on Amazon, Kobo, Scribd, Barnes & Noble, Apple platforms.

She founded Stress-Free Lifestyle Hub to help Stressful Individuals, Business Executives, Students, and any individual suffering from stress due to low self-esteem, low productivity, concentration crisis, and life dissatisfaction. She enables them to gain peace of mind by the blended knowledge and tools of quantum science & eastern philosophy.

The vision and mission of the Stress-free lifestyle hub founded by her are to establish an inner power-centric education system that can create a generation of productive & stress-free individuals.

The vision behind all her efforts is to highlight the need of life-oriented education, to create scientific consciousness about the 'Self' through education, to bring life close to nature, to tune life with cosmic rhythm, to challenge fast lifestyle, and to find out scientific and sustainable ways to establish peace and harmony in the world to make the world a better place to live in.

She is a member of 'Religion for Peace' (RFP), United Nations Plaza New York and Editorial Board Member of IJBST Group of Journals. Her present area of research is' Universal Consciousness & Quantum Reality.'
